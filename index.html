What is SDG #5?

Gender Equality is when the state in which access to rights or opportunities is unaffected by gender.


To further understand SDG #5, this video from the United Nations' YouTube channel offers well phrased and concise explanation.



There are many organizations working towards this Sustainable Development Goal. Some of these are:

  • UN Women

  • Equality Now

  • Vital Voices

  • UNDP

  • UNICEF

  • WHO
